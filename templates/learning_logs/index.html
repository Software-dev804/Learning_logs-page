Learning Log

Learning Log helps you keep track of your learning, for any topic you're learning about.

"What am I building? What am I breaking? What am I learning?" This site serves as a public accountability log and personal reference guide. Dive into the archives to see the evolution of your progress.

Features

The platform provide several Features:

Control

Users will be able to edit their entries personally.A user can do the editing unlimited times without interferance.This is advantagers for doing correction during reviews.

Privacy

Each user is able to own his/her entities and each one of them will only see their entries.User will edit his/her own entries and not even the developer will view the work.

Integrity

The site ensures that each users information remain how it is from the time of registering, loging in, creating their topics and entries and throught your journey in the site.

How does it work?

1 Start:

Create an account in the site to enable you track your learning smoothly with little effort. Click register to create an account andlog_in into your account to get started.

2 Create topics:

After creating an account, go ahead and create as many topics as you can in your personal account, add entries to the topic seemlessly as you learn them. How is it!!

3 Track your progress:

Create your logs to your liking to enable you keep a straight forward learning and allow you review,revisit and reference your scripts at any time.
